About Us

Tow Zone Alerts (TZA) is being created by civic technologists who came together in the Fall of 2022 over the shared goal of mitigating the nuisance of car towing. We build digital tools that notify car owners about tow zones. All of our services are free. We are currently partnering with cities in the greater Boston area to pilot our services.
